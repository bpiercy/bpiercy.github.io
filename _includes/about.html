Brandon D. Piercy

Materials Scientist

Hello! I am interested in designing new materials and devices using stuff we encounter every day. I tackle problems from every field, but am particularly interested in replacing rare elements with earth-abundant ones. I use a broad range of synthetic techniques, like vacuum processing, air-free chemistry, and electrochemistry, to create new materials with interesting properties. In the spirit of materials science, I use a host of characterization methods with a focus on spectroscopic methods to understand what properties my materials have and why they have certain behaviors.
